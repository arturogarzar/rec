Philosophy of Ministry

Our Mission

God has called River’s Edge Church to be a Multicultural Biblical community that goes into the world to Show Compassion and Share the Gospel.

Unified Body

We seek to be a UNIFIED BODY of English and Spanish speaking Christ followers dedicated to Walking Together in life, Growing Together as Disciple Makers, and Serving Together with our time, spiritual gifts, and physical resources.

Sacred Gatherings

We meet together regularly in SACRED GATHERINGS to Pray, Worship, Learn and Train, desiring together a clearer perspective on life and reality through the knowledge of God and His Word.

Holy Calling

Our HOLY CALLING compels us to go out into the world as grace-givers who love people with action in the name of Christ, and as disciple makers who proclaim the hope of salvation through the Gospel.
